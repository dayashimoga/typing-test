How to Improve Your Typing Speed

🎯 Focus on Accuracy

Speed comes with practice, but accuracy builds the foundation. Aim for 97%+ accuracy before pushing speed.

👆 Proper Finger Placement

Use the home row (ASDF JKL;) as your base. Each finger has assigned keys — learn them and your speed will skyrocket.

📅 Daily Practice

Just 10 minutes a day can improve your WPM by 20+ in a month. Consistency beats intensity.

👀 Don't Look Down

Train yourself to type without looking at the keyboard. This is the single biggest factor in typing speed improvement.
